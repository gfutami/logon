You are creating an account without a UCLA University ID (UID). Although usable with some UCLA applications, an account created without an UID is not generally eligible to receive campus services (email, network access). If you are a student, alumnus, faculty, staff, or contractor, please create a UCLA Logon with your UID to ensure access to university resources.
